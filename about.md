**Cult News** covers the movements that organize themselves around the end of the world. We take each seriously as a human phenomenon — neither credulous about their claims nor cruel to their members — and we try to answer the only question that survives every story: *why now, and why this?*

We are staffed by people who have, variously, studied these movements, reported on them, and in one case belonged to one. We think that last qualification is the most useful.

Every group we cover is invented. *Cult News* is the in-world press desk of **[The Herd of God](/herd/)**, an immersive theatrical production. The organizations, leaders, quotes, and figures on this site are fictional composites; any resemblance to a specific real person or group is not intended. The dread the site describes is, we would gently suggest, not fictional at all.

To reach the humans who make this — for press, casting, collaboration, or to ask whether any of it is real — [send a tip](/tips/), or write <a href="mailto:{{ site.production_email }}">{{ site.production_email }}</a>.
